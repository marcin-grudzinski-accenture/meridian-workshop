The Challenge

Where Meridian is today

  • ✕
    Reports module is broken. Known defects in filter behaviour, internationalization, and data patterns — unresolved at previous vendor contract end.
  • ✕
    No automated test coverage. Meridian IT has blocked further changes to the system. Nothing can ship safely.
  • ✕
    Missing operational capability. The operations team needs restocking recommendations — a core ask from VP Operations that the previous vendor never delivered.
  • ✕
    Minimal handoff documentation. The previous vendor's notes are incomplete. Meridian IT has no reliable picture of what they own.
